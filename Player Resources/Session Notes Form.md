# Submit Session Notes

After each session, submit your notes here. These are entirely your account of events.

**[Submit your notes for this session ->](YOUR-GOOGLE-FORM-URL)**

*Replace the link above with your Google Form URL. See SETUP.md for instructions on creating the form.*

---

## What the form asks for

- **Session number** -- which session you are submitting notes for
- **Character name** -- who you are playing
- **Session recap** -- what happened, in your own words
- **Theories and suspicions** -- what you think is actually going on; working theories, hunches, anything
- **Questions for the DM** -- things you want clarified or followed up on before next session

---

## Notes

- All fields except session number and character name are optional
- You can submit multiple times for the same session if you think of something later
- There are no wrong answers -- this is your read on events, not a test
